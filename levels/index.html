Stackable Levels

Coordinator

Focus

Learns the system and follows clear steps. Steady, accurate work that supports the weekly rhythm.

The Work

Building confidence in core tools and contributing reliably to team tasks.

The Aim

Become a dependable contributor who others can count on for consistent output.

Associate

Focus

Manages a wider range of tasks with growing independence.

The Work

Noticing what needs to be done and understanding how each task supports learners, SMEs and the team.

The Aim

Show sound judgement in decisions and take ownership without needing to be directed.

Senior Associate

Focus

Owns defined outputs and keeps projects moving forward.

The Work

Solving problems quickly and producing accurate, organised assets.

The Aim

Improve workflow quality and reduce rework for others across the team.

Lead

Focus

Runs a defined area of work and makes independent decisions.

The Work

Setting standards, guiding others, and maintaining consistent quality.

The Aim

Deliver outcomes without supervision and actively support the weekly rhythm.

Head

Focus

Leads an entire function with a long-term view.

The Work

Planning, designing systems and building capability across the team.

The Aim

Partner with the Founder on priorities and maintain clear direction for the function.

Founder / C-Suite

Focus

Holds responsibility for mission, strategy and culture.

The Work

Aligning the team to support effective decisions and ensuring progress toward long-term goals.

The Aim

Serve the mission and the people it exists for — building something that lasts.
