Your first assessment

Here's exactly what happens — and what comes next

60 minutes with Cody. No pressure, no judgement. You leave with a clear picture of your body and the plan built around it.

1
Scan

Assess

Come in for 60 minutes in comfortable clothes. You move, bend and squat while we watch for misalignments and try light exercises — stopping the moment anything hurts. You leave with a written report showing where your body is today.

Questions

Things people ask before they book

I'm not fit enough — is it too early to start?
The scan is designed for exactly that. Cody meets you where you are — there's no "minimum fitness" to book. The whole point is to see what your body does today, then build a plan from that starting point.
What if something hurts during the scan?
We stop. The stop-at-pain rule is built in. Pain tells us something specific, and the report captures it rather than pushing through it.
Do I have to commit to a plan after?
No. You can take the report and train on your own. Plan, Support and Train are all separate opt-ins — add them when (and if) you're ready.
What should I wear to the scan?
Anything you can move comfortably in. You'll bend, squat and try light exercises, so nothing tight or restrictive. No special gear needed.
Can I train if I have an existing injury?
Yes. Most clients come in with something — old back pain, a dodgy knee, a shoulder niggle. The scan maps it so the plan works around it, not through it. If needed, Cody liaises with your physio.
What happens after 12 weeks?
A new scan. You see what's changed, what's next, and whether to keep the current plan, switch modes, or rebuild from scratch. The cycle continues as long as you want it.
