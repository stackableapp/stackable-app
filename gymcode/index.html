Your first assessment

Here's exactly what happens — and what comes next

60 minutes with Cody. No pressure, no judgement. You leave with a clear picture of your body and the plan built around it.

1
Scan

Assess

Come in for 60 minutes in comfortable clothes. You move, bend and squat while we watch for misalignments and try light exercises — stopping the moment anything hurts. You leave with a clear picture of where your body is today.

2
Build

Plan

We turn your scan into a 12-week program tailored to your body, goals, and schedule. Every session has a reason; every week builds on the last.

3
Run

Train

Run your plan anywhere that works — at home, at the gym, or with a trainer. Built around your life, not the other way round.
