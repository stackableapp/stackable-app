design notes
· no reply mechanism by design — facilitator responds through content, not direct messages (maintains anonymity and avoids de-anonymisation by context) · "addressed" status is optional — notifies the student that their question was seen, without revealing who asked it to anyone · no upvotes — collective endorsement removes the protection of anonymity · "embarrassed to ask" category is the most important — it signals shame-adjacent confusion the learner cannot articulate any other way · submission count shown to facilitator only — students do not see how many others submitted · persistent placement (sidebar or footer) is intentional — it should always be findable without drawing attention to the act of using it
